Tallying & Outcome

After the Rating phase has ended, anyone can trigger the tally. It aggregates the final state of all rating markets into a single decision on the thesis, and it is the one transaction that finishes a debate.

From the leaves to the thesis​

Tallying starts at the leaves of the tree and moves upward; a parent can only be tallied once all of its children have been. Each argument jjj carries a rating that blends its own market with its sub-debate, weighted by the stake behind each:

built from three ingredients:

  • Own approval aja_jaj​ — the argument's standing in its own market: the pro-share price derived from the final balance of its pro and con reserves. It is weighted by vjv_jvj​, the vote tokens staked on that market.
  • The sub-debate's correction DjD_jDj​ — the already-tallied ratings of the child arguments, each signed by its polarity (σk=−1\sigma_k = -1σk​=−1 for an attacking child) and weighted by Wk=vk+VkW_k = v_k + V_kWk​=vk​+Vk​, its whole subtree's stake. It is weighted by Vj=∑kWkV_j = \sum_k W_kVj​=∑k​Wk​, the stake of everything beneath.
  • Stake decides the mix — a childless argument keeps its full own approval; a heavily debated one is corrected in proportion to the stake that debate attracted. At its parent, an argument counts with its whole subtree's stake among its siblings — one token, one voice, at every level of the tree.

The outcome​

Ratings fold upward until the thesis — which has no market of its own — has absorbed the stake-weighted judgement of the entire tree. The debate confirms the thesis if that aggregate is positive, and objects otherwise. Once finished, shares redeem, authors claim their fees, and bounty claims open.

A signal, not an oracle​

The outcome is a credible deliberation signal, not a manipulation-proof oracle: an egalitarian game in which every verified human holds the same non-purchasable budget cannot out-secure an attacker whose external budget exceeds the internal stakes. Automation that consumes the outcome (e.g. a DAO spending funds on a confirmed thesis) must bring its own guardrails — bind to the exact thesis content, require a participation quorum and margin, timelock past the claim window, and keep a veto path.
