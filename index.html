deliberate

Structured debates on Ethereum — argument trees, rated by markets, tallied on-chain.

Get Started  Whitepaper

Trees of Arguments

We don't want to live in a world where we can only say yes or no. A debate is a tree: a thesis at the root, pro and con arguments beneath it, counter-arguments beneath those. The structure invites multiple perspectives and puts every claim next to its strongest objections.

Argument Markets

Every argument carries a rating market. Participants stake vote tokens on arguments being underrated or overrated and profit when the rating corrects their way — being right early is what pays, so ratings converge on the debate's honest reading.

Decentralized Decision-Making

When rating ends, anyone can trigger the tally: ratings fold from the leaves to the root, and the thesis is confirmed or objected. Everything runs in a smart contract on Ethereum — transparent, verifiable, and open for other contracts to consume.
